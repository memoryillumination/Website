Privacy Policy

How Memory Illumination collects, uses, and protects your information.

Memory Illumination is founded on the principle of helping people use photos for a personalized art engagement activity designed for individuals with cognitive impairment. We know that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy very seriously. By visiting the Memory Illumination website, registering with Memory Illumination, or using any Memory Illumination services, you are accepting and agree to comply with this policy.

This Privacy Policy covers Memory Illumination's treatment of personal information that Memory Illumination gathers when you are on the Memory Illumination website and when you use Memory Illumination services. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Memory Illumination does not own or control, or to individuals that Memory Illumination does not employ or manage.

Information Collected by Memory Illumination

We only collect personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our website or services. This information allows us to provide you with a customized and efficient experience.

Information You Provide To Us

We receive and temporarily store your photo for conversion purposes. Identifying information you enter on our website or provide to us in any other way will be held in your account. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of many of our features.

Registration: In order for you to use Memory Illumination services you must complete a registration form. As part of this registration form, we require certain personal information including your first name, last name, and your email address. Our collection of personal information from you is limited to information that we require in order to provide our services to you.

You have the right to edit, update, access, or transfer your personal information, or to delete your personal information if you are terminating your registration with Memory Illumination.

Automatic Information

We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. Memory Illumination and its authorized agents automatically receive and record certain "traffic data" on their server logs from your browser including your IP address, Memory Illumination cookie information, and the page you requested. Memory Illumination uses this traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends, and administer the website. This does not involve collecting any of your personal information.

Memory Illumination may collect and use information regarding the geographic location of your devices. If the location data contains your personal information, then Memory Illumination will treat this location data in accordance with this Policy.

Communications

Memory Illumination will never provide your email address to a third party without your explicit permission. Memory Illumination may send out emails with Memory Illumination-related news, products, offers, surveys, or promotions. We send these notifications to you based on receiving your consent. If you do not want to receive emails from us, please visit the "Email notifications" section of your User Profile and/or follow the "unsubscribe" instructions contained in each email message.

Please note that in all cases you will continue to receive all system emails (e.g. those regarding forgotten user passwords) and legal notices (e.g. updates to our policies) from us unless you terminate your registration.

Cookies

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our website are visited and by how many people. Memory Illumination cookies do not collect personal information.

Memory Illumination utilizes third-party services like Google Analytics to help analyze how our site is used. These services also use cookies to collect information about visitors to our site (including IP addresses) in an anonymous form that does not include personal information.

Analytics

Memory Illumination uses services like Google Analytics to help analyze and understand how our site is used, so that we can make Memory Illumination more useful and interesting to you. These tools capture data about how individual users are using our site, including event logs like page views and button click events, device type, and device configuration settings — but never personal information. These third-party services treat the analytics data in accordance with this privacy policy and Memory Illumination does not disclose your personal information to any third parties through our use of these services.

Sharing Your Information

Memory Illumination Personnel: Memory Illumination personnel and authorized consultants and/or contractors may have access to user information if necessary in the normal course of Memory Illumination business.

Business Transfers: In some cases, we may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transactions, user information is typically one of the business assets that is transferred. Moreover, if Memory Illumination, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred.

Protection of Memory Illumination and Others: We may release personal information when we believe in good faith that release is necessary to comply with a law; to enforce or apply our Terms of Use and other policies; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Memory Illumination, our personnel, our users, or others.

Service Providers: Memory Illumination may share user information with third-parties that help Memory Illumination provide services to you. Our Service Providers will be given access to your information as is reasonably necessary to provide our services. Memory Illumination requires Service Providers to protect your data in a manner consistent with this Policy.

Data Storage: Your encrypted personal information is stored on servers provided by a third-party cloud-based service provider that protects and stores your data in a manner consistent with this Policy.

We do not sell your information to anyone.

Keeping Your Account Secure

Your Memory Illumination account information is protected by an encrypted password for your privacy and security. Protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer by logging off once you have finished using a shared computer.

Only personnel who need personal information to perform a specific job are granted access to it. All of our personnel are kept up to date on our privacy and security practices.

Memory Illumination has also implemented the Invisible reCAPTCHA API to help protect our website from spam and abuse. The use of reCAPTCHA is subject to the Google Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Your Rights and Choices

You can always opt not to disclose information, but then you may be unable to use certain features on our website. By visiting your User Profile, you can correct, amend, add, or delete personal information on our website.

If you do not wish to receive email from us, please visit the Settings of your User Profile and/or follow the instructions contained in the unwanted email message. Please note that you will continue to receive all system emails and legal notices from us unless you terminate your registration.

Children Under 18

You must be 18 years and older to register to use the Memory Illumination website. Memory Illumination does not specifically collect information about children. If we learn that Memory Illumination has collected information from a child under the age of 18, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. We recommend that minors under the age of 18 ask and receive permission from a legal guardian before using Memory Illumination.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Memory Illumination may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time, at its sole discretion. Use of information we collect now is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is used. If we make changes to the Privacy Policy, we will notify you by posting an announcement on the Memory Illumination website.
